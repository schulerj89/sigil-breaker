# Character Dialogue Agent Notes

## Decision

Selected the name `Glyph`.

## Rationale

- One syllable and easy to shout in gameplay.
- Fits the rift/sigil identity while still sounding like a playable mascot name.
- Works in short catchphrases such as `[confident] Glyph at your service!`.

## Selected Lines

- `[confident] Glyph at your service!`
- `[excited] Rift sealed! Nice work!`
- `[laughs] That was slick!`
- `[determined] Keep moving, we got this!`
- `[playful] Sparks up, feet fast!`
- `[gasp] Oof! Reboot me!`
- `[dizzy] Stars... sigils... snacks?`

## Risks Caught

- Do not overuse long joke lines during combat because they will mask important SFX on mobile speakers.
- Keep visible transcript text available for user review before attaching these to gameplay events.
