# Commander Kade Visual Assessment

## Selected Design

Selected `commander-kade-concept-03-jackal-strategist.png`.

## Why

- Best commander read at mobile size: tall ears, strong teal/gold collar, and centered chest rift device make the silhouette readable in a small portrait panel.
- Distinct from Glyph while still matching the same amber fur, teal/cobalt tech, lime rift glow, and stylized mascot shape language.
- Most suitable for a flat hologram MVP because the face, ears, and shoulder shape remain clear when cropped to a comms-feed bust.

## Rejected Directions

- Concept 01 looked too close to a heroic field-player variant instead of a senior commander.
- Concept 02 had a useful veteran read but felt heavier and more realistic than the current title/hero style.
- Concept 04 read more like an engineer companion than the mission commander.
- Concept 05 was clean, but the mantle/pose was less visually decisive than concept 03.
