## Why Concept 002 Won

Concept 002 had the strongest balance of expressive mascot identity, readable mobile silhouette, clean rig-friendly body volumes, blaster-capable hands, and compatibility with Sigilbreaker's gunmetal/neon direction. It also avoided the more obvious orange-fox platform mascot look better than concept 001.

## Meshy Direction

Use the compact alien gadget-runner identity: warm tawny fur, large pointed ears, swept crest, confident muzzle, oversized gloved hands, athletic biped body, curved tail, short blue-black scarf, asymmetrical gunmetal gauntlet with cyan sigil core, dark tactical vest, chunky boots, and controlled orange/teal accents.

## Risks

- Push gunmetal armor and cyan/green sigil glow so the final character feels like Sigilbreaker, not a generic mascot.
- Keep scarf, tail, and gauntlet chunky and simple for mobile animation.
- Avoid direct similarity to existing platform mascots.
