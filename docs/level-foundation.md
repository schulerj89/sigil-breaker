# Foundation Level

The first playable FPS foundation level is a 34 x 34 unit map. Each character is one square meter in gameplay space. Traversal lanes are authored with at least three units of open space so mobile movement has room to breathe around walls and cover.

## Legend

- `#`: wall or boundary collision.
- `.`: walkable floor.
- `S`: player spawn.
- `C`: low cover collision.
- `E`: endpoint marker.
